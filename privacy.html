Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Finance4Fun ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website at finance4fun (the "Site").

We believe in transparency and keeping things simple — just like our approach to teaching finance.

Information We Collect

Currently, Finance4Fun is a static educational website. Here's what that means for your privacy:

  • No personal data collection — We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.
  • No account creation — There is no sign-up or login system at this time.
  • No cookies — We do not use tracking cookies or any advertising cookies.
  • No third-party analytics — We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools.

How Your Data Is Stored

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. The site uses your browser's localStorage for a small number of functional preferences (such as tracking whether you've used your free quiz preview). This data:

  • Stays entirely on your device — it is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data or using our built-in reset feature.
  • Contains no personal information — only simple flags like "free quiz used."

Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. While we don't collect data ourselves, GitHub may collect basic server logs (such as IP addresses) as part of their hosting service. You can review GitHub's Privacy Statement for more details.

We also load fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect basic usage data when loading fonts. You can review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Future Changes

As Finance4Fun grows, we may introduce features that involve collecting user data (such as membership accounts or a contact form). If and when that happens, we will update this Privacy Policy to reflect those changes and notify users accordingly.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data at this time, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. Once we begin collecting user information in the future, we will implement appropriate mechanisms for you to exercise your data rights.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page. We're in the process of setting up our contact channels and will have full contact information available soon.
